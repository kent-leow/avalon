# Avalon Game Rules Reference

## Official Game Rules Summary

### Player Counts and Character Distribution
- **5 Players**: 3 Good, 2 Evil
- **6 Players**: 4 Good, 2 Evil  
- **7 Players**: 4 Good, 3 Evil
- **8 Players**: 5 Good, 3 Evil
- **9 Players**: 6 Good, 3 Evil
- **10 Players**: 6 Good, 4 Evil

### Mission Team Sizes
| Players | Mission 1 | Mission 2 | Mission 3 | Mission 4 | Mission 5 |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| 5       | 2         | 3         | 2         | 3         | 3         |
| 6       | 2         | 3         | 4         | 3         | 4         |
| 7       | 2         | 3         | 3         | 4         | 4         |
| 8       | 3         | 4         | 4         | 5         | 5         |
| 9       | 3         | 4         | 4         | 5         | 5         |
| 10      | 3         | 4         | 4         | 5         | 5         |

### Special Rules
- **Two-Fail Rule**: Mission 4 requires 2 fail votes to fail when playing with 7+ players
- **Five Rejections**: If 5 consecutive team proposals are rejected, Evil wins immediately
- **Assassin Victory**: If Good wins 3 missions, the Assassin can win by correctly identifying Merlin

### Character Abilities
- **Merlin**: Sees all Evil players except Mordred
- **Percival**: Sees Merlin and Morgana but cannot distinguish between them
- **Morgana**: Appears as Merlin to Percival, is Evil
- **Mordred**: Evil player hidden from Merlin's sight
- **Oberon**: Evil player who doesn't know other Evil players, and they don't know him
- **Assassin**: Can attempt to kill Merlin if Good wins 3 missions

### Character Dependencies
- **Merlin** must be included if **Percival** is selected
- **Morgana** must be included if **Percival** is selected
- **Assassin** must be included if **Merlin** is selected
- **Mordred** can only be included if **Merlin** is present

### Game Flow
1. **Role Assignment**: Characters randomly assigned to players
2. **Role Reveal**: Players privately learn their roles and information
3. **Mission Rounds**: 5 missions total, first team to win 3 missions wins
4. **Team Proposal**: Leader proposes team for current mission
5. **Team Vote**: All players vote to approve or reject the team
6. **Mission Execution**: Selected team members vote for mission success/failure
7. **Mission Result**: Mission succeeds if no fail votes (except Mission 4 with 7+ players)
8. **Assassin Phase**: If Good wins 3 missions, Assassin attempts to identify Merlin

### Victory Conditions
- **Good Wins**: Complete 3 missions successfully AND Assassin fails to identify Merlin
- **Evil Wins**: 3 missions fail OR 5 consecutive team rejections OR Assassin correctly identifies Merlin
